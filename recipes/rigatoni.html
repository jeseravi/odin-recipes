Rigatoni

Ingredients

  • 1 box of rigatoni pasta
  • 1 pound ground mild sausage
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 5 tbsp heavy whipping cream
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 pinch parmesan

Steps

  • Oil in the pan, make it hot. Start cooking pasta
  • Chop up the garlic clove and throw it in the pan
  • Once the garlic is browned add in the ground mild sausage
  • Add in the crushed tomatoes and whipping cream
  • Drain cooked pasta and add it to the pan
  • Serve and garmish with parmesan. Enjoy!
