Fettuccine

Ingredients

  • 16 oz fettuccini pasta
  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 1 pinch parsley
  • 1 tbsp chili flakes
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 cups parmsean
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream

Steps

  • Oil in the pan, make it hot. Start cooking pasta
  • Chop up the garlic clove and throw it in the pan
  • Once the garlic is browned add in the remaining incredients
  • Drain cooked pasta and add it to the pan
  • Serve and enjoy!
