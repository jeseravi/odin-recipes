Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 16 oz fettuccini pasta
  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups parmsean
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream

Steps

  • Oil in the pan, make it hot. Start cooking pasta
  • Chop up the garlic cloves and chicken and throw em in the pan
  • Once the chicken is cooked add in the remaining incredients
  • Mix in cooked pasta
  • Enjoy!
